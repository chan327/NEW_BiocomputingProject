About Aspirin

(Description) Aspirin is a commonly used medication to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and lower fever. It belongs to a class of drugs known as nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs). Additionally, aspirin is often used in low doses to reduce the risk of heart attacks, strokes, and blood clot formation.
